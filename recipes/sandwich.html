Sandiwch

Description

A sandwich is a versatile dish typically consisting of meat, cheese, vegetables, and spreads placed between two or more slices of bread. It was named after the 18th-century Earl of Sandwich, who popularized the convenient, portable meal

It is a dish typically consisting variously of meat, cheese, sauces, and vegetables used as a filling between slices of bread, or placed atop a slice of bread; or, more generally, any dish in which bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side each bread slice.
  3. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side down, in the hot skillet; top with 1 slice cheese.
  4. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side up, on top cheese.
  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  6. Repeat with remaining bread and cheese slices. Serve and enjoy!s
