Maggi

Description

Maggi is India's most beloved instant noodle dish. Quick, comforting, and endlessly customizable, it has been a staple snack for students and families alike for decades. Whether eaten plain or jazzed up with vegetables and spices, Maggi is always the answer

Ingredients

  • ! Maggi packet
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Maggi Masala(included in the paclet)
  • Optional: chopped onions, tomatoes, green chillies

Steps

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water
  2. Add Maggi noodles and masala powder
  3. Cook for 2 minutes stirring occasionally
  4. Remove from heat and serve hot
